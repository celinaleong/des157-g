Career Public Relations

Bio:
Mia Wilson is a 25 years old woman working in Public Relations in Southern California. Her job is often high stress and does not give her much time to take care of her mental health. She likes to go to the beach to relax, meditate, and listen to the waves. It helps her slow her breathing and calm down.


Motivations:
Find ways to meditate without having to escape somewhere far
Use small breaks in her day to her advantage
Confront the difficulties in caring for mental health and professional/social/other obligations


Frustrations:
There is too little time in the day to meditate

Environment:
She is often surrounded by a fast-paced work place and newly-formed social circle. She doesn't feel like she has that much of an outlet to vent her emotions or talk about anxiety. Working in PR, social media is a huge part of her life--sometimes this affects her anxiety yet she can't avoid it.


Technical Familiarity:
Being a millenial, she is very familiar with technology and hopes to be able to incorporate the good parts of it into her daily meditation routine.
